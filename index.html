Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our consultation process.

How long is a consultation session?

A standard psychological consultation session lasts for 50 minutes. The initial assessment session may take up to 60 minutes to gather comprehensive background information.

Are sessions confidential?

Absolutely. Confidentiality is a core ethical principle in psychology. Everything discussed in your session remains strictly private, except in rare legal circumstances involving imminent harm, which will be discussed during your first session.

How do I join the video session?

Once you book an appointment, you will receive an email confirmation containing a secure link. Simply click the link 5 minutes before your scheduled time to enter the virtual waiting room.

When will in-person sessions be available?

We are finalizing our clinic setup and expect to open for in-person visiting sessions within the next two months. Updates will be posted on this website, and current clients will be notified directly.
